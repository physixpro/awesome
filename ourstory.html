Our Story



What started as a humble 6 table diner back in the 1950's in the heart of New York City; lim's kitchen has since grown to be the number one ranked, internationally recognized five star restaurant in the world to serve Asian American fusion dishes.

"JungHyun Lim," an American born citizen with Korean roots had this to say when asked about her culinary style.

" Growing up I always wanted to showcase the best that my heritage has to offer, and at the same time show gratitude to the country which gave my family so many opportunities." Her dishes have been rated 5 stars by even the hardest critics of asian/american cusine.
